Uses and human interaction

Finding out how many pandas exist in the wild is not an easy task. In fact, it requires a massive effort, with teams of researchers spending many hours trekking through steep, mountainous bamboo forests looking for telltale signs. In particular, for dung! Often, they have to walk for days, inspecting every metre of the forest floor for panda poop! When they find some, they sift through it and pick out any pieces of undigested bamboo.
